RULEBOOK

Read carefully

  • Do you want to be a pokemaster? Let's set upon the journey....
  • The game consists of 30 questions.
  • Time limit for the game is 2 hours only.
  • You are firstly supposed to select a pokemon of your choice to play the game.
  • The selected pokemon will be in the initail evolved stage with a 0 XP(Experience Points).
  • You are provided 1000 coins initially.
  • The game consists of a map where you will find 15 pokestops and 15 pokegyms.
  • Pokestops are represented by pokeballs whereas pokegyms are represented by wifi signals.
  • Each Pokestop has an entry fee of 100 coins whereas Pokegym has an entry fee of 200 coins.
  • A battle awaits you at every pokestop and pokegym.
  • You can win the battle by giving a correct answer to the question given there.
  • Remember, Pokestops have comparatively easier questions than Pokegyms.
  • After answering the question, you have to attack the opponent pokemon to see whether you have won or lost the battle.
  • Winning the battle against the opponent will award you some coins.
  • The increase in your pokemon XP and coins on winning the battle depends on the battle itself. For example, if you have a fire type pokemon, winning a battle against a water type pokemon would be difficult, hence you will be awarded more coins and XP whereas winning a battle against a grass pokemon would be easier, hence you will be awarded lesser coins and XP. Winning a pokegym will award you more points than winning a pokegym.
  • Your pokemon will evolve to the next stage after your XP goes beyond 4000 and 8000 which will reduce the entry fees for the pokestops and the pokegyms.
  • After winning, the corresponding pokestop or pokegym will be disabled and you can visit the other stop or gym.
  • On giving a wrong answer to the question, you lose the battle against the opponent pokemon and your pokemon will faint.
  • Losing the battle will have no effect on your pokemon's XP.
  • You need to visit pokecenter and spend some coins to rejuvenate your pokemon every time it faints, otherwise you cant play further.
  • Your can again visit the same pokestop or pokegym even after losing the fight.
  • But remember, Maximum number of visits allowed is only 3.
  • The number of visits for every pokestop or pokegym is indicated by its color.
  • Pokestop:
    Red: 0 visit
    Green: 1 visit
    Blue: 2 visits
    Black: 3 visits(disabled)
  • Pokegym:
    Blue: 0 visit
    Pink: 1 visit
    Yellow: 2 visits
    Black: 3 visits(disabled)
  • After finishing the game, you can log out, otherwise you will be automatically logged out after the game finishes.
  • Let's begin....
